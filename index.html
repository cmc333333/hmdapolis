The epic "just for fun" game... not grounded in reality! our lawyers made us say this

HMDApolis was inspired by and uses data from the Home Mortgage Disclosure Act.

Here's how to play: Each round a new profile is generated using HMDA data. Use this profile to to help you compete against your opponent to capture the most cities. Capture a city by having the most accepted loans around it by the end of the game.
